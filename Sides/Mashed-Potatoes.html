Creamy Roasted Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
  • Servings: 6 servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 3 lbs Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt or sour cream
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil or butter
  • 1/2 to 1 cup warm milk
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh chives or parsley for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Boil: Place potatoes and garlic cloves in a large pot. Cover with cold water by 1 inch and add a generous pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15–20 minutes until tender.
  2. Drain: Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Let them steam for a minute to dry out.
  3. Mash: Use a potato masher to break down the potatoes and garlic.
  4. Mix: Stir in the yogurt, olive oil (or butter), and 1/2 cup of the warm milk. Continue mashing until smooth, adding more milk as needed to reach your desired consistency.
  5. Season: Season generously with salt and pepper. Top with fresh herbs and a drizzle of oil before serving.

📝 Notes

Pro Tip: Using Yukon Gold potatoes is the secret to naturally buttery and creamy mash without needing as much fat as Russets!
