Crispy Air Fryer Brussels Sprouts

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Temperature: 205°C (400°F)
  • Servings: 4 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • Optional: Balsamic glaze or lemon wedges

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat: Preheat the air fryer to 205°C (400°F).
  2. Toss: Mix the sprouts with oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl until coated.
  3. Fry: Place in the basket in a single layer. Air fry for 12-15 minutes.
  4. Shake: Give the basket a good shake halfway through to ensure even browning.
  5. Finish: Serve hot once the outer leaves are dark and crispy.

📝 Notes

The Secret: Don't overcrowd the basket! If the sprouts are piled up, they will steam instead of crisping.
