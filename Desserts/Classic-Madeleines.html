Classic French Madeleines

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 30 minutes (includes chilling)
  • Servings: 24 Madeleines

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract (optional)
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest (optional)
  • Confectioners' sugar, for dusting

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Beat: In a large bowl, beat the eggs and granulated sugar with an electric mixer until the mixture is thick and pale (about 5-8 minutes).
  2. Flavor: Stir in the vanilla extract, almond extract, and lemon zest.
  3. Fold Flour: Sift the flour and salt over the egg mixture. Using a spatula, gently fold the flour into the batter until just combined.
  4. Fold Butter: Take a small amount of the batter and stir it into the melted butter. Then, gently fold the butter mixture back into the main batter.
  5. Chill: Cover the batter and refrigerate for at least 30-60 minutes. Chilling the batter is key to getting the classic "hump" on the back!
  6. Prep Pan: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Generously butter and flour a madeleine pan.
  7. Bake: Spoon the batter into the molds (do not overfill). Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are golden and the tops spring back.
  8. Finish: Invert the pan onto a rack to cool. Dust with confectioners' sugar once cooled.

📝 Notes

Texture Tip: Madeleines are best enjoyed the same day they are baked. They pair perfectly with a cup of tea or coffee!
