Skillet Cookie Sundae

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes
  • Servings: 8 servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Toppings: Vanilla ice cream, chocolate syrup, caramel sauce, and whipped cream

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream: In a large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until fluffy. Beat in the vanilla and egg.
  3. Dry Mix: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Combine: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Press: Spread the dough evenly into a 10-inch oven-safe skillet (cast iron works best).
  6. Bake: Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown but the center is still slightly soft.
  7. Serve: While still warm, top with generous scoops of vanilla ice cream and drizzle with chocolate and caramel sauce.

📝 Notes

Pro Tip: For an extra gooey experience, take the skillet out just as the edges turn golden. The residual heat from the cast iron will finish the center without over-baking it.

Recipe by Ree Drummond (The Pioneer Woman)
