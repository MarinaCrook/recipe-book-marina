Classic Lemon Bars

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Bake Time: 50 minutes
  • Total Time: 4 hours (includes chilling)
  • Servings: 16-20 Bars

🛒 Ingredients

Shortbread Crust

  • 1 cup (226g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups + 2 tbsp (265g) all-purpose flour

Lemon Filling

  • 2 cups (400g) granulated sugar
  • 6 tbsp (46g) all-purpose flour
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup (240ml) fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tsp fresh lemon zest
  • Confectioners’ sugar for dusting

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 325°F. Line a 9x13-inch glass pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides.
  2. Crust: Mix melted butter, sugar, vanilla, and salt. Add flour and stir until combined. Press firmly into the pan. Bake for 20-22 mins. Once out, poke holes in the warm crust with a fork.
  3. Filling: Sift sugar and flour together. Whisk in eggs, lemon juice, and zest until smooth.
  4. Bake: Pour filling over the warm crust. Bake for 22-26 mins until the center is set.
  5. Chill: Cool at room temperature for 2 hours, then refrigerate for 1-2 hours.
  6. Serve: Lift out using parchment paper, dust with powdered sugar, and slice.

📝 Notes

Storage: Lemon bars stay fresh in the refrigerator for up to 1 week. They can also be frozen for up to 3 months.

Pro Tip: For the cleanest edges, wipe your knife with a damp cloth after every single slice!
