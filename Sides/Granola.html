Crispy Healthy Homemade Granola

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
  • Yield: About 8 cups

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 ½ cups raw nuts and/or seeds
  • 1 tsp fine-grain sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup melted coconut oil or olive oil
  • ½ cup maple syrup or honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional: ⅔ cup dried fruit or chocolate chips

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 350°F and line a large rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix Dry: In a large mixing bowl, combine the oats, nuts/seeds, salt, and cinnamon.
  3. Mix Wet: Pour in the oil, maple syrup, and vanilla. Mix well until every oat is lightly coated.
  4. Bake: Pour the granola onto the pan and spread it into an even layer. Bake for 21 to 24 minutes, stirring halfway through.
  5. Cool: Let the granola cool **completely** before breaking it into chunks. This is the secret to those big clumps!
  6. Add-ins: Stir in your dried fruit or chocolate chips once cooled.

📝 Notes

The Secret to Clumps: Press the granola down with a spatula after stirring halfway through, and do not touch it until it is 100% cool!
