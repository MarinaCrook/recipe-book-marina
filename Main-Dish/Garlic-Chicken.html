Creamy Garlic Chicken

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
  • Servings: 4 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (halved/filleted)
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt & 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil & 1 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Dredge: Season the flour with salt and pepper. Lightly coat the chicken pieces in the flour, shaking off any excess.
  2. Sear: Heat oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken for about 5 minutes per side until golden. Remove to a plate.
  3. Garlic: Reduce heat to medium. Add the minced garlic to the pan and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Deglaze: Add the chicken broth. Use a spatula to scrape up the flavorful brown bits (fond) from the bottom of the pan.
  5. Simmer: Stir in the heavy cream and oregano. Let it simmer for a few minutes until slightly thickened.
  6. Finish: Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted. Return the chicken to the skillet, coat with sauce, and serve!

📝 Notes

Storage: Store leftovers in an airtight container for up to 3 days. Reheat gently on the stove with a splash of water or broth to loosen the sauce.
