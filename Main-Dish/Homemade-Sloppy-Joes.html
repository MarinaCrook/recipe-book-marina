Homemade Sloppy Joes

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
  • Servings: 6-8 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1/2 yellow onion, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 6-8 toasted hamburger buns

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Sear: Brown the beef, onion, and bell pepper in a large skillet. Drain the grease.
  2. Aromatics: Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Sauce: Stir in ketchup, water, brown sugar, mustard, chili powder, and Worcestershire sauce.
  4. Simmer: Cook on low for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. Serve: Pile onto toasted buns and serve warm.

📝 Notes

Make it Spicy: Add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the simmer for an extra kick!
