Spiced Crispy Roasted Chickpeas

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: 35 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes
  • Servings: 4 snacks

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas (garbanzo beans)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp cumin

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 400°F. Drain and rinse the chickpeas.
  2. Dry: Pat the chickpeas very dry with a clean kitchen towel. Any moisture left will prevent them from getting truly crunchy!
  3. Roast: Toss chickpeas with olive oil and salt. Spread in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake: Roast for 30–35 minutes, shaking the pan every 10 minutes.
  5. Season: While warm, toss with the remaining spices.

📝 Notes

Flavor Variation: You can easily swap the paprika and cumin for chili powder or even cinnamon sugar for a sweet version.
