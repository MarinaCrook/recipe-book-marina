Soft & Chewy M&M Cookies

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Bake Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 55 minutes (includes chilling)
  • Servings: 24 Cookies

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup M&Ms (plus extra for topping)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Cream: Beat the butter and both sugars until light and fluffy. Add the egg and vanilla, mixing until well combined.
  2. Mix: Gradually add the flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix on low speed until just combined. Fold in the M&Ms by hand.
  3. Chill: Chill the dough in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes. This prevents the cookies from spreading too thin!
  4. Prep: Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  5. Scoop: Scoop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the sheet. Press a few extra M&Ms onto the top of each dough ball for a professional look.
  6. Bake: Bake for 9–11 minutes. The edges should be set, but the centers should still look slightly soft.
  7. Cool: Let cookies cool on the pan for 5 minutes before moving to a wire rack.

📝 Notes

Don't overbake! They might look a little underdone in the middle when you pull them out, but they will firm up as they cool, keeping that "soft and chewy" texture.
