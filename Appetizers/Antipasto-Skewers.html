Antipasto Skewers

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Marinade Time: 2 hours
  • Total Time: 2 hours 20 minutes
  • Servings: 12 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 12 mozzarella balls (cocktail sized)
  • 5 tablespoons Italian dressing (divided)
  • 12 cherry tomatoes
  • 12 pitted olives
  • 24 refrigerated cheese tortellini
  • 12 marinated artichokes
  • 24 slices salami
  • 12 slices roasted red pepper (about 1" each)
  • Chopped fresh parsley (garnish, optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Marinate Cheese: Combine mozzarella balls and 2 tablespoons Italian dressing. Marinate at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Prep Pasta: Cook tortellini according to package directions. Drain and rinse under cold water. Toss with remaining Italian dressing and marinate for 1 hour.
  3. Assemble: Thread items on 6" wooden skewers, alternating colors and textures.
  4. Store/Serve: Cover and refrigerate for up to 24 hours. Sprinkle with herbs and drizzle with balsamic reduction just before serving if desired.

📝 Notes

Storage: Keep covered and refrigerated until ready to serve, for up to 24 hours.

Recipe adapted from Holly Nilsson at Spend with Pennies
