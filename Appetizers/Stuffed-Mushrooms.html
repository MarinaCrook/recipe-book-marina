Stuffed Mushrooms

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Bake Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
  • Servings: 8 servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb baby mushrooms (rinsed)
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 garlic cloves (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup plain breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely grated Parmesan (plus extra for topping)
  • 4 oz cream cheese (softened)
  • 2 Tbsp fresh parsley (finely chopped)
  • 1 Tbsp fresh thyme (finely chopped)
  • Cooking spray, salt, and black pepper

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 400°F. Grease a baking sheet. Remove stems from mushrooms and finely chop them; arrange the caps on the baking sheet.
  2. Sauté Stems: Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Cook the chopped stems until moisture evaporates (about 5 mins). Add garlic for 1 minute, then stir in breadcrumbs for 3 minutes until toasted. Season and let cool.
  3. Mix Filling: In a large bowl, combine the breadcrumb mixture with Parmesan, cream cheese, parsley, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Stuff: Fill each mushroom cap generously with the mixture and sprinkle with additional Parmesan.
  5. Bake: Bake for 20 minutes until the mushrooms are soft and the tops are golden brown. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

📝 Notes

Storage: These are best served hot, but leftovers can be kept in an airtight container in the refrigerator for 3 to 4 days.

Recipe adapted from Makinze Gore at Delish
