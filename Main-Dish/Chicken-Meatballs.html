Skillet Chicken Parm Meatballs

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes
  • Servings: 4 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Mix: Combine ground chicken, panko, parmesan, egg, garlic, oregano, and salt in a bowl. Mix gently until just combined.
  2. Shape: Form the mixture into 12 to 14 meatballs (about 1.5 inches each).
  3. Sear: Heat olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Brown the meatballs on all sides (about 8-10 minutes total). They don't need to be cooked through yet.
  4. Sauce: Pour the marinara sauce around the meatballs in the skillet. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Cheese: Sprinkle the mozzarella over the meatballs.
  6. Bake: Transfer the skillet to a preheated 400°F oven. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the cheese is melted and meatballs are cooked through.
  7. Serve: Top with fresh basil and serve straight from the skillet!

📝 Notes

Oven-Safe: Make sure your skillet is oven-safe (like cast iron or stainless steel). If not, transfer everything to a baking dish before the "Bake" step.
