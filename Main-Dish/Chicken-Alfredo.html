Classic Chicken Alfredo

Recipe Details

  • Total Time: 30 minutes
  • Servings: 4 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 8 ounces dried fettuccine noodles
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (about 1 pound total)
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, divided
  • 2 tablespoons neutral oil
  • 8 tablespoons (1 stick) European-style unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook fettuccine until al dente (8-10 mins). Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water, then drain.
  2. Pat chicken dry. Season with 3/4 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper.
  3. Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Cook chicken until golden-brown (5-7 mins per side) until it hits 165°F.
  4. Let chicken rest for 3 minutes, then slice into 1/2-inch thick pieces.
  5. In the same pan, melt remaining butter. Sauté minced garlic until fragrant (30-60 seconds).
  6. Whisk in heavy cream, Parmesan, nutmeg, and remaining salt/pepper. Simmer for 3-4 minutes. Use reserved pasta water to thin if needed.
  7. Toss pasta in the sauce, top with chicken, and garnish with parsley.

📝 Notes

Storage: Refrigerate in an airtight container for up to 3 days. Reheat gently on the stovetop.
