Crispy Baked Sweet Potato Fries

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes (plus soaking)
  • Cook Time: 25 minutes
  • Temperature: 215°C (425°F)
  • Servings: 4 servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes (peeled and sliced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt & black pepper to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Soak: Cut potatoes into 1/4-inch strips. Soak in cold water for 30 mins, then pat completely dry.
  2. Preheat: Heat oven to 215°C. Line a pan with parchment paper.
  3. Coat: Toss dry fries with cornstarch. Add oil and spices; toss again to coat evenly.
  4. Bake: Spread in a single layer (don't crowd!). Bake for 20-25 mins, flipping halfway through.
  5. Finish: Season with salt immediately after removing from the oven.

📝 Design Notes

Pro-Tip: Using a dark forest green (#2d4a2a) for headers provides a nice contrast to the bright orange of the sweet potatoes!
