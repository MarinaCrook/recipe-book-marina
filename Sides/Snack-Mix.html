Sweet and Salty No-Bake Snack Mix

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 12 servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 5 cups toasted oat cereal (like Cheerios)
  • 2 cups pretzel twists or sticks
  • 1 cup dry roasted peanuts
  • 1 cup candy-coated chocolate pieces (like M&Ms)
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup honey or corn syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Mix Dry: In a very large bowl, combine the cereal, pretzels, peanuts, and chocolate candies.
  2. Combine Wet: In a microwave-safe bowl, whisk together the melted butter, peanut butter, and honey until smooth. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. Coat: Pour the peanut butter mixture over the cereal mixture. Toss gently with a large spatula until everything is evenly coated.
  4. Set: Spread the mixture out on parchment paper to let it cool and set for about 10-20 minutes before serving.

📝 Notes

Customization: Feel free to swap the peanuts for almonds or add mini marshmallows for a "s'mores" vibe!
