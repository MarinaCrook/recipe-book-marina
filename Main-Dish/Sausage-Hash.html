Baked Sausage Breakfast Hash

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Bake Time: 57 minutes
  • Total Time: 72 minutes
  • Servings: 4-5 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

Seasoned Potatoes

  • 1.6lb potatoes, peeled, cut into 2cm cubes
  • 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp cooking salt

Everything Else

  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 100g / 4 oz streaky bacon
  • 1 lb good pork sausages (casings removed)
  • Eggs (as many as desired)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 200°C / 400°F.
  2. Potatoes: Toss potatoes with oil and seasoning. Spread on a lined tray and bake for 15 minutes.
  3. Veg: Toss bell pepper and onion with oil/salt/pepper. Add to the tray with potatoes.
  4. Meat: Squeeze dollops of sausage meat from casings onto the tray. Scatter with bacon pieces.
  5. Bake: Bake for 20 minutes, toss everything, then bake for another 15 minutes until potatoes are soft.
  6. Eggs: Lower oven to 180°C/350°F. Make wells in the hash and crack eggs in. Bake for 7 mins or until eggs are done.
  7. Serve: Garnish with parsley and serve hot!

📝 Notes

1. Potato Type: Avoid waxy potatoes; starchier types get fluffier in the oven.

2. Storage: Keeps for 3 days in the fridge. Best reheated in a pan to keep the potatoes crisp.
