Beef and Cheese Manicotti

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Bake Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 60 minutes
  • Servings: 7 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 8 oz. manicotti shells
  • 3-4 cups marinara sauce
  • 8 oz. mozzarella cheese (shredded)

The Stuffing

  • 1/2 medium yellow onion (diced)
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp onion powder & 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 4 oz. mozzarella & 4 oz. parmesan cheese (shredded)
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 cups baby spinach (cooked and patted dry)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Cook Beef: In a Dutch Oven, brown ground beef with onions, salt, pepper, onion powder, and oregano (7-8 mins). Add garlic for 1 min. Set aside to cool.
  2. Mix Cheese: In a medium bowl, combine ricotta, mozzarella, parmesan, egg, salt, and pepper.
  3. Sauté Spinach: Wilt baby spinach in a nonstick skillet with a little olive oil. Pat dry.
  4. Combine: Stir the wilted spinach and cooked beef into the cheese filling.
  5. Prep Pasta: Boil manicotti until al dente. Strain and lay on a clean towel.
  6. Fill: Add filling to a zip-top bag, snip the corner, and pipe the mixture into the shells.
  7. Layer: Spread 1/2 cup sauce in a 9x13 dish. Arrange stuffed shells in a single layer.
  8. Topping: Pour remaining sauce over the shells and top with mozzarella.
  9. Bake: Cover with foil at 350°F for 30 mins. Uncover and bake 10 mins more.

📝 Notes

Storage: Keep in an airtight container in the fridge for 3-4 days.

Make Ahead: This dish freezes beautifully! Thaw in the fridge before reheating.
