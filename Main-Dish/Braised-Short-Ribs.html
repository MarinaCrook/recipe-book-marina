Braised Short Ribs

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 25 minutes
  • Bake Time: 3 hours 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 3 hours 55 minutes
  • Servings: 8 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 6 bone-in short ribs (about 5 3/4 pounds)
  • Kosher salt
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 large Spanish onion (1/2-inch pieces)
  • 2 ribs celery (1/2-inch pieces)
  • 2 carrots (peeled and cut into 1/2-inch pieces)
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 1/2 cups tomato paste
  • 2 to 3 cups hearty red wine
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 bunch fresh thyme (tied with string)
  • 2 bay leaves

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Sear: Season ribs with salt. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Brown ribs 2-3 mins per side (work in batches). Remove ribs from pan.
  2. Veggie Paste: Preheat oven to 375°F. Puree onion, celery, carrots, and garlic in a food processor.
  3. Brown Aromatics: Drain fat from the pot, add fresh oil, and brown veggie paste until very dark (5-7 mins). Stir in tomato paste and brown for 4-5 mins.
  4. Reduce: Add wine and scrape the bottom of the pan. Reduce the liquid by half.
  5. Braise: Return ribs to the pan. Add water until meat is almost covered. Add thyme and bay leaves.
  6. Cook: Cover and bake for 3 hours. Turn ribs halfway through. Remove lid for the last 20 mins to brown and reduce sauce. Serve tender meat with braising liquid.

📝 Notes

Flavor Secret: Searing the short ribs in batches builds a deep crust and flavor "fond" in the pan that makes the sauce incredible.
