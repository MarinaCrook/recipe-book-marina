Easy Homemade Caramel Popcorn

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 1 hour
  • Total Time: 1 hour 15 minutes
  • Servings: 10 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup corn syrup
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 5-6 quarts popped popcorn

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat: Heat oven to 250°F (120°C). Place popped popcorn in large shallow baking pans.
  2. Boil: Combine brown sugar, butter, corn syrup, and salt in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
  3. Simmer: Let it boil without stirring for 5 minutes.
  4. Vanilla: Remove from heat; stir in baking soda and vanilla.
  5. Coat: Pour over popcorn and toss until evenly coated.
  6. Bake: Bake for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
  7. Cool: Let cool completely before breaking into pieces.

📝 Notes

Storage: Store in an airtight container to keep it from getting sticky. It stays fresh for up to a week!
