Classic Philly Cheesesteak

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
  • Servings: 2 Sandwiches

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ribeye steak, very thinly sliced
  • 1/2 yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 slices Provolone cheese
  • 2 Hoagie rolls
  • 2 tbsp butter, softened
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Garlic salt and black pepper

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Caramelize: Sauté the onions in olive oil until soft and golden. Set them aside.
  2. Sear: Sauté the thinly sliced beef over high heat until browned. Season with garlic salt and pepper.
  3. Melt: Mix the onions back in, divide into two piles, and top each with provolone slices until melted.
  4. Toast: Butter your hoagie rolls and toast them until golden.
  5. Load: Scoop the meat and cheese into the rolls and serve hot.

📝 Notes

The Meat: For the best results, have your butcher slice the ribeye for you, or partially freeze it at home to get those ultra-thin slices yourself!
