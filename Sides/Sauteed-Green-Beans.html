Garlic Sauteed Garden-Fresh Green Beans

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 4 servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 lb fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt (to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Optional: A squeeze of lemon juice

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Blanch: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add green beans and cook for 3-4 minutes until tender-crisp. Drain and immediately shock in ice water to keep them bright green.
  2. Dry: Pat the beans thoroughly dry with a towel.
  3. Sauté: In a large skillet, heat olive oil and butter over medium-high heat.
  4. Flavor: Add the green beans and sauté for 2-3 minutes. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 more minute until fragrant (don't let the garlic burn!).
  5. Season: Season with salt and pepper. Toss well and serve immediately.

📝 Notes

Texture Tip: Shocking the beans in ice water is the secret to that "restaurant-style" bright green color and snappy texture.
