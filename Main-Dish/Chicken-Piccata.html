Chicken Piccata

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
  • Servings: 4 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 skinless and boneless chicken breasts (halved/butterflied)
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • All-purpose flour (for dredging)
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 5 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup brined capers, rinsed
  • 1/3 cup fresh parsley, chopped

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Season & Flour: Season the chicken with salt and pepper. Dredge each piece in flour and shake off the excess.
  2. Sear Batch 1: In a large skillet over medium-high heat, melt 2 tbsp butter with 3 tbsp olive oil. When it sizzles, cook two chicken pieces for 3 mins per side until golden. Set aside.
  3. Sear Batch 2: Add another 2 tbsp butter and 2 tbsp olive oil to the pan and cook the remaining chicken. Set aside.
  4. Deglaze: Add lemon juice, chicken stock, and capers to the skillet. Bring to a boil, scraping the bottom of the pan to release the flavor bits.
  5. Simmer: Return all chicken to the pan and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Finish: Remove chicken to a platter. Whisk the final 2 tbsp of butter into the sauce until smooth. Pour over chicken and top with parsley.

📝 Notes

Flavor Balance: If the sauce is too tart, add an extra splash of chicken stock. If you like it extra "lemony," add some fresh zest at the very end!
