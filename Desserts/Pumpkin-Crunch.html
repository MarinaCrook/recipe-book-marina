Pumpkin Crunch

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Bake Time: 50 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 5 minutes
  • Servings: 12 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 package yellow cake mix
  • 1 can pumpkin (15 oz)
  • 1 can evaporated milk (12 oz)
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 cup butter, melted

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prep the Pan: Grease the bottom of a 9x13 pan and line the bottom with parchment or wax paper (this is critical for the flip!).
  2. Mix Filling: In a large bowl, whisk together the pumpkin, evaporated milk, eggs, sugar, cinnamon, and salt until well combined.
  3. Layer: Pour the pumpkin mixture into the pan. Sprinkle the dry yellow cake mix evenly over the top.
  4. Top & Bake: Sprinkle the chopped pecans over the cake mix, then drizzle the melted butter evenly over everything. Bake at 350°F for 50-55 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. The Flip: Let it cool completely. Run a knife around the edges to loosen. Invert the pan onto a large platter so the pecans are now on the bottom and the pumpkin is on top.
  6. Serve: Top with whipped cream and enjoy!

📝 Notes

Perfect for: Holiday parties or family gatherings. It's actually even better the next day after the flavors have chilled!
