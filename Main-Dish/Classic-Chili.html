Classic Beef Chili

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 1 hour
  • Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes
  • Servings: 6 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Brown: In a large pot, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat until browned. Drain the grease.
  2. Aromatics: Add the onion and bell pepper. Sauté for 5 minutes until soft. Stir in the garlic for 1 minute.
  3. Spice: Stir in the chili powder, cumin, and paprika. Cook for 1-2 minutes to let the spices "bloom."
  4. Simmer: Add the tomatoes, beans, and broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 45-60 minutes.
  5. Finish: Season with salt and pepper. Serve with cheese, sour cream, and jalapeños.

📝 Notes

Pro Tip: For extra heat, add a chopped jalapeño with the onions or a dash of cayenne pepper!
