Shortcut Chicken Pot Pie

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes
  • Servings: 6 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 lb cooked rotisserie chicken, shredded or diced
  • 1 bag (12 oz) frozen mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 1 can (10.5 oz) cream of chicken soup
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder & 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 package refrigerated pie crusts (2 count) OR 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat: Preheat your oven to 400°F.
  2. Mix Filling: In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, frozen vegetables, cream of chicken soup, chicken broth, and seasonings. Stir until well mixed.
  3. Prepare Pan: Line a 9-inch pie plate with one of the refrigerated pie crusts.
  4. Fill: Pour the chicken mixture into the pie crust and spread it evenly.
  5. Top: Place the second pie crust on top. Pinch the edges together to seal them. Cut a few small slits in the top crust to let steam escape.
  6. Wash: Brush the top of the crust with a lightly beaten egg for a golden finish.
  7. Bake: Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  8. Rest: Let it sit for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.

📝 Notes

Variations: If you prefer a lighter top, use a sheet of puff pastry instead of a second pie crust. Just drape it over the top and trim the edges!
