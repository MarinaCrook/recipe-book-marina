Lemon Garlic Grilled Broccolini

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: 6 minutes
  • Total Time: 11 minutes
  • Servings: 4 servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 bunches broccolini (about 1 lb)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 lemon (half for juice, half for wedges)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prep the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (about 400°F).
  2. Season: In a large bowl or on a baking sheet, toss the broccolini with olive oil, salt, pepper, and garlic powder until evenly coated.
  3. Grill: Place the broccolini directly on the grates. Grill for 3 minutes per side, or until the stems are tender-crisp and the tops are slightly charred.
  4. Finish: Remove from the grill and immediately squeeze the fresh lemon juice over the hot broccolini.
  5. Serve: Serve hot with extra lemon wedges on the side.

📝 Notes

Broccoli vs Broccolini: Broccolini is more delicate than standard broccoli, so it cooks much faster on the grill. Watch it closely to ensure the leafy tops don't burn!
