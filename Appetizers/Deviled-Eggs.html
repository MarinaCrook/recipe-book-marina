Devilled Eggs

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooling Time: 5 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
  • Servings: 18-20 pieces

🛒 Ingredients

  • 12 large eggs
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp mayonnaise
  • 1/4 heaped teaspoon cooking salt
  • 50g (3 tbsp) cold unsalted butter
  • Paprika (for garnish)
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped chives (for garnish)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Boil: Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Gently lower eggs in. Reduce heat slightly to prevent cracking and boil for 10 minutes.
  2. Swirl: Swirl the water every minute for the first 6 minutes. This centrifugal force helps center the yolks.
  3. Peel: Transfer eggs to cold water for 5 minutes. Tap the base and peel under water for ease.
  4. Prep Whites: Cut eggs in half and remove yolks with a teaspoon. Select the best 18-20 halves.
  5. The Filling: Blitz yolks, mustard, vinegar, mayo, and salt in a food processor. Add cold butter and blitz until creamy and blended.
  6. Assemble: Pipe the filling into the egg white halves using a star nozzle.
  7. Garnish: Sprinkle with paprika and chives. Serve at room temperature for the best texture.

📝 Notes

Make Ahead: These keep well for up to 2 days. For the creamiest texture, remove them from the fridge 30 minutes before serving.

Recipe adapted from Nagi Maehashi at RecipeTin Eats
