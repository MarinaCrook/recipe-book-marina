Spicy Edamame

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: 5 minutes
  • Infusion Time: 5 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 2-4 servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 bag (10 oz) frozen edamame in the pod
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 teaspoons sriracha
  • ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 tablespoon low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • Flaky sea salt (for serving)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Boil: Bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add edamame and cook for 4-5 minutes. Turn off heat, let stand for 1 minute, then drain.
  2. Infuse Oil: In a small skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat until shimmering. Remove from heat and stir in minced garlic, sriracha, and red pepper flakes. Let sit for 5 minutes to infuse.
  3. Whisk Sauce: Whisk the soy sauce, honey, and lime juice into the garlic oil mixture.
  4. Toss: Add the drained edamame to the sauce and toss gently to coat every pod.
  5. Serve: Transfer to a dish, sprinkle with flaky sea salt, and serve immediately.

📝 Notes

Storage: Keep in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days. Reheat in a microwave or a pan over low heat until warmed through.

Recipe adapted from Yumna Jawad at Feel Good Foodie
