Perfect Grilled Filet Mignon

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes (plus 30 min resting)
  • Cook Time: 12 minutes
  • Total Time: 22 minutes
  • Servings: 4 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 (6-8 oz) filet mignon steaks, 2 inches thick
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt
  • 2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Temper: Bring steaks to room temperature for 30 minutes before grilling.
  2. Prep: Preheat grill to high heat (450°F+). Clean and oil the grates.
  3. Season: Pat steaks dry, brush with oil, and season generously with salt and pepper.
  4. Grill: Sear for 5-6 minutes per side for medium-rare (internal temp 130°F-135°F).
  5. Rest: Remove from heat, top with butter, and tent with foil. Let rest for 10 minutes.

📝 Notes

Pro Tip: Using an instant-read thermometer is the best way to ensure your steak is cooked exactly to your preference.
