Decorated Sugar Cookies

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Bake Time: 10 minutes
  • Chill Time: 1 hour
  • Total Time: 1 hour 40 minutes
  • Servings: 30 Cookies

🛒 Ingredients

For the Cookies:

  • 200g unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 150g granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 400g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt

For the Royal Icing:

  • 2 egg whites (or meringue powder equivalent)
  • 400g powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • Food coloring as desired

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Cream: Beat the butter and sugar until creamy. Add the egg and vanilla, mixing until fully incorporated.
  2. Dough: Sift in the flour and salt. Mix until a dough forms. Wrap in plastic and chill for at least 1 hour.
  3. Roll & Cut: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Roll the dough on a floured surface to 1/4 inch thickness. Use cookie cutters to create shapes.
  4. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are just barely golden. Let cool completely before decorating.
  5. Icing: Whisk egg whites with lemon juice until frothy. Gradually add powdered sugar until thick. Divide and add food coloring.
  6. Decorate: Use a piping bag to outline and "flood" the cookies. Let the icing dry completely (several hours) before stacking.

📝 Notes

Pro Tip: If your dough gets too warm while cutting shapes, pop it back in the fridge for 10 minutes. This helps the cookies keep their clean edges in the oven!

Recipe by Lorena Salinas (Cravings Journal)
