Garlic Butter Shrimp

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 4 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs large shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1.5 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth or dry white wine
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • Lemon wedges for serving

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Sear: Heat olive oil and 2 tbsp of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add shrimp in a single layer, season with salt and pepper, and cook for 2 minutes per side until opaque. Remove shrimp to a plate.
  2. Sauce: Melt the remaining 2 tbsp of butter in the same pan. Sauté the garlic for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Deglaze: Pour in the broth and lemon juice. Simmer for 2 minutes until the liquid reduces slightly.
  4. Combine: Stir in the parsley, return the shrimp to the pan, and toss to coat. Serve immediately.

📝 Notes

Serving: This pairs beautifully with a side of pasta or crusty bread to soak up the garlic butter sauce.
