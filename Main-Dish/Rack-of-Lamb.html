Herb-Crusted Rack of Lamb

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes
  • Servings: 2-4 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 (1 1/4-lb) racks of lamb, fat trimmed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Marinate: Whisk together the garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, pepper, and olive oil. Rub the mixture thoroughly over the lamb racks.
  2. Roast: Preheat oven to 450°F. Place lamb, fat side up, on a roasting rack. Roast for 20-25 minutes.
  3. Temp: Aim for 130°F on an instant-read thermometer for medium-rare.
  4. Rest: Transfer to a board and let rest for 10 minutes to allow juices to redistribute.
  5. Carve: Slice between the rib bones and serve immediately.

📝 Notes

Serving Tip: This pairs beautifully with roasted root vegetables or a fresh mint chimichurri!
