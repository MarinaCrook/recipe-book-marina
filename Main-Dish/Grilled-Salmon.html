Easy Grilled Salmon

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 4 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 (6-ounce) salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Fresh herbs for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Marinate: Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and Dijon together. Let the salmon sit in the mixture for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat: Preheat your grill to medium-high and lightly oil the grates.
  3. Grill: Place salmon skin-side down. Cook for 4-5 minutes with the lid closed.
  4. Flip: Flip carefully and cook for another 3 minutes until it reaches your desired doneness.

📝 Notes

Serving: This salmon is delicious served alongside grilled asparagus or a fresh summer salad.
