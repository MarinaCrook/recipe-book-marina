Baked Ham and Cheese Sliders

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
  • Servings: 24 sliders

🛒 Ingredients

  • ¾ cup melted butter
  • 1 ½ tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 ½ teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 ½ tablespoons poppy seeds
  • 1 tablespoon dried minced onion
  • 24 mini sandwich rolls
  • 1 pound thinly sliced cooked deli ham
  • 1 pound thinly sliced Swiss cheese

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Glaze: In a small bowl, whisk together the melted butter, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, poppy seeds, and dried onion.
  3. Layer: Slice the rolls in half. Place the bottom halves in the prepared baking dish. Layer half the ham, followed by all of the Swiss cheese, and then the remaining ham.
  4. Top and Pour: Place the top halves of the rolls over the ham and cheese. Pour the butter mixture evenly over the top of the sliders.
  5. Bake: Bake for 20 minutes, or until the rolls are lightly toasted and the cheese is completely melted.
  6. Serve: Slice between the rolls to separate and serve warm.

📝 Notes

Storage: Leftover sliders can be stored in the refrigerator for up to two days. Reheat covered at 350°F until warmed through.
