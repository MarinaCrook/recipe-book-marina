Greek Salad

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 4 servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 English cucumber (sliced/seeded)
  • 1 green bell pepper (chopped into 1-inch pieces)
  • 2 cups halved cherry tomatoes
  • 5 ounces feta cheese (cut into ½ inch cubes)
  • ⅓ cup thinly sliced red onion
  • ⅓ cup pitted Kalamata olives
  • ⅓ cup fresh mint leaves

🍶 Dressing

  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • ¼ teaspoon sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Method: Whisk all ingredients together in a small bowl until well combined.

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Arrange: On a large platter or in a salad bowl, arrange the cucumber, green pepper, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, red onions, and olives.
  2. Dress: Drizzle with the prepared dressing and very gently toss.
  3. Garnish: Sprinkle with extra oregano and top with fresh mint leaves.
  4. Serve: Season with extra pepper to taste and serve immediately.

📝 Notes

Feta: For the best flavor, look for feta sold in brine rather than pre-crumbled varieties.

Recipe adapted from Love and Lemons
