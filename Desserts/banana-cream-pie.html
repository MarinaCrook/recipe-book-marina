Banana Cream Pie

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Bake Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes
  • Servings: 8-10 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 10 tbsp salted butter, melted
  • 2 ⅔ cups crushed Nilla Wafers (one 11oz box)
  • 4 to 5 medium bananas
  • 2 large eggs + 2 egg yolks (room temp)
  • 4 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract + pinch of sea salt
  • 1 cup heavy cream + 1 cup whole milk

👩‍🍳 Instructions

The Crust

  1. Preheat oven to 275°F. Grease an 8 or 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom.
  2. Crush Nilla wafers into fine crumbs. Mix with melted butter until moistened.
  3. Press firmly into the bottom and sides of the pan. Use a flat-bottomed glass to pack it down tightly.

The Custard

  1. Whisk eggs, yolks, sugar, cornstarch, salt, and vanilla until smooth.
  2. Heat 1 cup cream and 1 cup milk in a saucepan until simmering.
  3. Temper: Slowly drizzle hot milk into the egg mixture while whisking constantly. Pour everything back into the saucepan.
  4. Cook on low, whisking for about 5 mins until thickened. Strain if lumpy.

Assembly & Baking

  1. Line the crust with slices of two bananas. Pour warm custard over them.
  2. Bake for 30 minutes (center should be slightly jiggly).
  3. Cool at room temperature, then refrigerate loosely covered overnight.

Serving Day

  1. Whip 1 ½ cups heavy cream with powdered sugar and vanilla to medium peaks.
  2. Top the pie with two more sliced bananas and a cloud of whipped cream.
  3. Garnish with dark chocolate shavings.

📝 Notes

Timing: Wait until serving day to add the final banana layer and whipped cream to keep them fresh.

Storage: Keep refrigerated for up to 2 days. Do not freeze!
