Perfectly Grilled Corn on the Cob

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10-15 minutes
  • Total Time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 4-6 ears

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 to 6 ears of fresh corn, shucked
  • Extra-virgin olive oil or butter
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Optional toppings: Lime wedges, chili powder, cilantro, or feta cheese

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Oil: Brush each ear of corn lightly with olive oil or melted butter.
  3. Grill: Place the corn directly on the grill grates. Grill for 10 to 15 minutes, turning every 2–3 minutes, until the kernels are charred in spots and tender.
  4. Season: Remove from the grill and season immediately with salt, pepper, and any extra toppings you enjoy.

📝 Notes

Variations: For Mexican Street Corn (Elote), brush with mayo and top with cotija cheese, chili powder, and lime after grilling!
