Easy Homemade Coleslaw

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Chill Time: 1 hour
  • Total Time: 1 hour 15 minutes
  • Servings: 8 servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 1/2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar (or more to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp celery salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 bag (14 oz) coleslaw mix (shredded cabbage and carrots)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Whisk Dressing: In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, sugar, celery salt, and pepper until smooth.
  2. Combine: Add the coleslaw mix to the bowl and toss thoroughly until every bit of cabbage is coated in the dressing.
  3. Chill: For the best flavor, cover and refrigerate for at least one hour before serving. This allows the cabbage to soften slightly and the flavors to meld.
  4. Serve: Give it a quick toss again just before serving.

📝 Notes

Pro Tip: If you prefer a thinner dressing, you can add a splash of milk or buttermilk. If you like it extra tangy, add another teaspoon of vinegar!
