Easy Apple Strudel

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 35 minutes
  • Total Time: 55 minutes
  • Servings: 8 servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 sheet puff pastry (thawed)
  • 3 large apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp), peeled and sliced
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)
  • 1/4 cup raisins (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 large egg (beaten with 1 tbsp water for egg wash)
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Prepare Filling: In a large bowl, toss the sliced apples with sugar, cinnamon, lemon juice, walnuts, raisins, and flour until well coated.
  3. Roll Pastry: On a lightly floured surface, roll out the puff pastry sheet into a 10x12-inch rectangle.
  4. Fill: Spoon the apple mixture down the center of the pastry rectangle, leaving about an inch at the ends.
  5. Fold: Fold the sides of the pastry over the filling, overlapping them slightly. Tuck the ends under and pinch to seal.
  6. Vent & Glaze: Carefully transfer the strudel to the prepared baking sheet. Cut several small slits in the top for steam to escape. Brush the top with the egg wash.
  7. Bake: Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the pastry is puffed and deep golden brown.
  8. Cool & Serve: Let cool for at least 15 minutes. Dust with powdered sugar and serve warm, ideally with vanilla ice cream.

📝 Notes

Top Tip: Make sure your puff pastry is cold when it goes into the oven to get the flakiest layers. If the dough gets too soft while you are filling it, pop the whole tray in the fridge for 10 minutes before baking.
