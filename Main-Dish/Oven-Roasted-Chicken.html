Crispy Roasted Half Chicken

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 35 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes
  • Servings: 2 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (approx. 4 lbs), halved lengthwise
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried thyme

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Dry: Pat the chicken skin bone-dry with paper towels for maximum crispiness.
  2. Season: Rub with olive oil, then apply the herb and spice mixture evenly over the skin and underside.
  3. Roast: Place skin-side up on a parchment-lined pan at 425°F. Roast for 35–45 minutes.
  4. Rest: Once it hits an internal temp of 165°F, remove from oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before carving.

📝 Notes

Serving: Goes great with roasted asparagus or mashed potatoes for a complete meal.
