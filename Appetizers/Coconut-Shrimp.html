Air Fryer Coconut Shrimp

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 12 minutes
  • Total Time: 22 minutes
  • Servings: 4 servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 pound large raw shrimp (peeled/deveined, tails attached)
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt (more to taste)
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¾ cup shredded unsweetened coconut
  • ¼ cup panko breadcrumbs
  • Cooking spray
  • Sweet chili sauce (optional, for serving)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat: Preheat your air fryer to 360°F. Once heated, spray the basket with cooking spray.
  2. Station Setup: Set up three shallow bowls. 1: Flour, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. 2: Whisked eggs. 3: Coconut and panko breadcrumbs mixed.
  3. Bread the Shrimp: Dip each shrimp into the flour (shake off excess), then the egg, then the coconut/panko mixture. Press gently to make sure the coating sticks.
  4. Air Fry: Arrange shrimp in the basket without touching. Spray the tops. Cook for 6 minutes, flip, spray again, and cook for 4-6 more minutes until golden.
  5. Finish: Season with a pinch of salt while hot and serve with sweet chili sauce.

📝 Notes

Storage: Store in an airtight container for up to 3 days in the fridge. Reheat in the air fryer to maintain crispiness.

Freezing: Can be frozen for up to 3 months. Thaw in the fridge overnight and reheat in a 350°F oven or air fryer.

Recipe adapted from Yumna Jawad at Feel Good Foodie
