Essential Fluffy Pancakes

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 8-10 Pancakes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Mix Dry: Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Mix Wet: In a separate bowl, whisk milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine: Pour wet ingredients into dry and stir until just combined (some lumps are fine!).
  4. Griddle: Pour 1/4 cup portions onto a hot, greased skillet.
  5. Flip: Flip when bubbles appear on the surface and edges look set. Cook until golden brown.

📝 Notes

Serving: Top with pure maple syrup, fresh berries, or a dusting of powdered sugar.
