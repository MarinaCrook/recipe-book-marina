Cherry Delight

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour 20 minutes (includes chilling)
  • Servings: 9 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

The Crust

  • 1-1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 6 tablespoons butter, melted

The Filling

  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 carton (8 ounces) frozen whipped topping, thawed (3 cups)
  • 1 can (21 ounces) cherry pie filling

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Make the Crust: In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, confectioners' sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture firmly onto the bottom of a greased 8-inch square pan.
  2. Mix the Filling: In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and confectioners' sugar until completely smooth. Gently fold in the whipped topping.
  3. Layer: Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly over the graham cracker crust. Spoon the cherry pie filling over the top.
  4. Set: Refrigerate until set, at least 1 hour before serving.

📝 Notes

Make Ahead: This dessert is actually better the next day! The crust softens slightly and the flavors meld together beautifully.

Storage: Store covered in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
