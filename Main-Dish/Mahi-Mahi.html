Lemon Butter Mahi-Mahi

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 4 Servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 (6-oz) Mahi-Mahi fillets
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Season: Pat the fillets dry and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high. Cook fillets 3-4 minutes per side until golden and opaque. Remove to a plate.
  3. Sauce: Melt butter in the pan, sauté garlic for 1 minute, then stir in lemon juice and zest.
  4. Serve: Spoon the garlic butter over the fillets and top with fresh parsley.

📝 Notes

Serving: This pairs wonderfully with wild rice or a light garden salad.
