Classic Ploughman's Sandwich

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 10 minutes
  • Servings: 1 Sandwich

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 thick slices of crusty sourdough or bloomer bread
  • 1 tbsp softened unsalted butter
  • 2 tsp Branston pickle or chunky fruit chutney
  • 2-3 oz extra-sharp Cheddar cheese, sliced
  • 1/2 tart green apple, thinly sliced
  • A handful of fresh arugula or watercress

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Butter: Spread the butter on both slices of bread. This keeps the bread from getting soggy.
  2. Spread: Apply the Branston pickle or chutney to the bottom slice.
  3. Layer: Place the sharp cheddar slices down, followed by the thin apple slices.
  4. Greens: Top with the fresh arugula for a peppery crunch.
  5. Serve: Close the sandwich and slice in half.

📝 Notes

Texture: Using a high-quality, crusty bread makes all the difference here to support the heavy layers of cheese and pickle.
