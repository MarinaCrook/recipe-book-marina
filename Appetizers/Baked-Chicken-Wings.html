Baked Chicken Wings

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes
  • Servings: 8 servings

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 pounds chicken wings
  • 2 Tablespoons baking powder (must be aluminum-free)
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prep Oven: Adjust oven rack to the upper-middle position. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Prepare Tray: Line a baking sheet with foil and place a wire rack on top. Spray the rack with non-stick spray.
  3. Dry Wings: Use paper towels to pat the wings dry. It is essential to dry them thoroughly for maximum crispiness!
  4. Season: Combine salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and baking powder. Sprinkle over wings and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Arrange: Place wings, skin side up, in a single layer on the wire rack.
  6. Bake: Bake for about 40-60 minutes, turning every 20 minutes, until browned and crispy.
  7. Finish: Let stand for 5 minutes, then toss with your prepared sauce.

🌶️ Buffalo Sauce

  • 1/3 cup Frank's Wings Hot Sauce
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon water

Method: In a saucepan over medium heat, stir ingredients until sugar dissolves. Let cool to room temperature before tossing with wings.

📝 Notes

Baking Powder: You MUST use aluminum-free baking POWDER (not soda). Regular baking powder with aluminum can leave a metallic aftertaste.

Recipe adapted from Lauren Allen at Tastes Better From Scratch
